## Full description

ReceiptVault is the private document scanner for Android. Scan receipts, bills, invoices, warranties, IDs, and contracts — everything stays encrypted on your device. No account. No cloud upload. No ads.

**Quick scan**
- Home screen widget and Quick Settings tile
- App shortcuts: Smart Scan and Batch Scan
- Share images or PDFs into ReceiptVault from any app

**Smart Scan**
- Live smart frame with in-app camera
- Auto-enhance and perspective correction (Pro)
- Document type detection: receipt, bill, invoice, warranty, ID, contract
- Confidence chips auto-fill merchant, total, tax, and date
- Multi-page and ID card (front + back) capture (Pro)

**Your encrypted vault**
- SQLCipher encrypted database
- Biometric app lock
- Screenshot blocking
- Search inside scanned text (Pro)

**Pro features**
- Batch scan sessions
- Export PDF, JPEG, PNG, TXT, ZIP (Pro)
- Multi-page PDF export
- Smart folder routing
- Encrypted backup and restore
- Google Play subscription or lifetime license key

**Pricing (launch promo through Sep 2026)**
- Monthly $1.99 · Yearly $11.99 · Lifetime $24.99 on Play
- Lifetime license keys (sideload) $31.99 — 20% below standard Play price

Receipt content is never uploaded. Network is used only for license validation and legal pages.
