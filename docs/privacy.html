Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026

ReceiptVault is a privacy-first, fully offline Android application. This policy explains what data the app handles on your device.

Summary

  • No account required
  • No network access for core features
  • Receipts and photos stay on your device in an encrypted database
  • No analytics or ad tracking in the MVP

Data on your device

Receipt details, optional photos, folders, settings, and Pro entitlement (via Google Play) are stored locally and encrypted with SQLCipher.
