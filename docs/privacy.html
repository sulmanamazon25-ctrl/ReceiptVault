Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026

ReceiptVault is a privacy-first Android application. Receipts and documents stay on your device; limited network use applies only to license activation, legal pages, and Google Play.

Summary

  • No account required for core use
  • Receipts, bills, IDs, and other scans stay on your device in an encrypted database
  • Smart Scan and OCR run entirely on-device — content is never uploaded
  • License activation sends a device identifier hash and IP address for fraud prevention
  • No analytics or ad tracking

Data on your device

Receipt details, optional photos, folders, settings, and Pro entitlement (Google Play or license key) are stored locally and encrypted with SQLCipher.

License keys

When you activate a license key, we store a hashed device identifier and your IP address on our server to enforce one-device-per-key policy. Receipt content is never uploaded.

Google Play purchases

Payments are processed by Google. See Google's Privacy Policy.
